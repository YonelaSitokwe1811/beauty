How to use or apply Minola products

1 HAIRFOOD
250ml Enriched with 100% organic coconut oil & Shea butter . Directions to use: Apply on your to the scalp and style as desired.
2 HAIR GROWTH OIL
100ml Enriched with neem oil, argan , almond oil & karkar oil. Directions to use For rich growth: Apply on your scalp.
3 HAIR SPRITZER
250ml Prevents hair breakage. Directions to use: Shake before use , spritz hair to the roots.

50g Directions to use: make a paste with water, Apply to your hair, Cover with shower cap/ plastic for 2hrs, ,Rinse it off ,Apply Minola hair growth oil.
5 HAIR SHAMPOO
200ml Enriched with black soap & essential oils . Anti dandruff Directions to use: Wet your hair, Apply a bit of shampoo to your wet , rub as it forms lather , massage scalp then rinse.
